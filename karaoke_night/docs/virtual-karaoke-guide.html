What You Need

If you can watch a video on your device you're ready to sing — That's it!

📱Smartphone, tablet, or laptop
📶WiFi connection (recommended over cellular)
🔊Speaker or TV for the backing track
🎵Your backing track ready to go (KARAOKE NIGHT, YouTube, or whatever you use)
🎤Your voice — no experience required!
💡 Tip: Connect to WiFi before joining — it gives you a much more stable connection than cellular during a video call.
Join the Session

Your host will send you a link before the event — Zoom, WhatsApp, or whatever platform the group is using. Just tap the link and you're in.

📱 iPhone / iPad

  1. Install Zoom from the App Store if needed
  2. Tap the link your host sent
  3. Tap Join with Video
  4. Tap Join with Computer Audio
  5. Allow mic and camera when prompted

🤖 Android

  1. Install Zoom from Google Play if needed
  2. Tap the link your host sent
  3. Tap Join with Video
  4. Tap Join with Device Audio
  5. Allow mic and camera when prompted
💡 Tip: No Zoom account needed to join — just tap the link and go. If Zoom is already installed, make sure it is updated first.

📱 iPhone / iPad

  1. Make sure WhatsApp is installed and updated
  2. Tap the video call link or answer the group call
  3. Allow mic and camera when prompted
  4. You're in — no extra steps needed

🤖 Android

  1. Make sure WhatsApp is installed and updated
  2. Tap the video call link or answer the group call
  3. Allow mic and camera when prompted
  4. You're in — no extra steps needed
💡 Tip: WhatsApp video calls work great on mobile. For larger groups, make sure your host uses the Group Video Call feature.

Your Turn to Sing

When it's your room's turn, you control your own show. The host coordinates the order but you run your own playback.

1
Host gives you the heads up
Listen for your name or watch the group chat — your host will let you know you're up next.
2
Unmute yourself
Tap the microphone icon to unmute. Make sure your device mic can pick up your voice.
3
Start your backing track
Play from KARAOKE NIGHT, YouTube, or whatever source you use. Crank the volume so it fills your room and carries through your mic to everyone else.
4
Sing!
No judgement zone. Own it. 🎤
5
Mute yourself when done
Tap the microphone icon again. This keeps background noise down while others are singing.
💡 Tip: Prop your phone or tablet up so your hands are free. Position it close enough that your voice comes through clearly over the backing track.
Audio Tips

A little setup goes a long way. These tips make a big difference for everyone on the call.

🎤 When Singing

  • Unmute before you start
  • Back track volume up — let it fill the room
  • Hold or prop device at voice height
  • Minimize background noise in your space
  • Mute again when done

👂 When Listening

  • Stay muted — it helps everyone
  • Use reactions to cheer (👏 🎉)
  • Keep your own background noise low
  • Unmute briefly to clap or cheer between songs
💡 Echo? If others hear an echo from your room, lower your speaker volume slightly or use headphones. The mic is picking up the audio from your speakers and sending it back out.
💡 Zoom users: Go to Settings → Audio and set Noise Suppression to Low or Off — this keeps the backing track from being filtered out.
Playing Backing Tracks — Browser Guide

If you are playing your backing track from a laptop browser and want to cast or share it to a TV or bigger screen, browser choice matters.

✅
Chrome
Best — native Chromecast support built in
✅
Edge
Works — Chromecast via menu → More Tools → Cast
❌
Firefox
No Chromecast on desktop — use Chrome or Edge instead. On iOS, Firefox can cast via AirPlay.
⚠️
Safari
AirPlay only — works with Apple TV, not Chromecast
💡 Mobile — KARAOKE NIGHT: Open KARAOKE NIGHT in your mobile browser — Chrome on Android, Safari or Firefox on iPhone all work great. Find your song and tap to play. To cast to a TV, use the Cast icon in Chrome (Android) or AirPlay (iPhone).
💡 Just singing along? If you are not casting to a TV and just playing audio through your device speakers, any browser works fine.
❤️ Not using KARAOKE NIGHT? Sorry to hear that but you are always welcome at the party! Just use your preferred karaoke player and join in — we'd love to see you!
Troubleshooting

Something not working? Try these first.

ProblemFix
Can't hear anythingCheck device volume. Tap the speaker icon in your app. Make sure you joined with audio enabled.
Others can't hear meCheck you are not muted — tap the microphone icon. Check the app has microphone permission in your phone settings.
Echo or feedbackTurn your volume down a bit or use headphones. Someone in the call has their speaker too loud near their mic.
Video is frozenLeave the meeting and rejoin using the same link from your host.
Call cuts off at 40 minNormal on free Zoom. Your host will start a new meeting — watch for a new link in the group chat.
Can't find the linkCheck your texts and email. Ask your host to resend it in the group chat.
Backing track sounds muffledTurn it up louder in your room. Try Zoom's Noise Suppression set to Low or Off in audio settings.
